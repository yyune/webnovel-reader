Chapter 1: The Beginning

It was a quiet morning when Elara stepped out into the mist. The cobblestone streets of Arlowe were still wet from the night's rain, and the scent of damp earth filled the air.

She clutched the letter tighter in her hand. A letter that promised change, danger, and perhaps a new beginning.

"To the one who seeks truth, follow the stars beyond the mountain," it read.

Elara took a deep breath and stepped forward.

The journey had begun.
